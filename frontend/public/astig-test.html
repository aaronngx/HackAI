Step 3 — Find Astigmatic Axis

Two blue circles will appear on screen at the same time — one on top, one below.
Each circle contains striped lines at a slightly different angle.

Choose which circle's lines look sharper or clearer to you:

● Press 1 — Upper circle looks clearer
● Press 2 — Lower circle looks clearer
● Press 3 — Both look the same

There are no right or wrong answers — trust your first instinct.

~6 comparisons · Head still · Circles use blue tripole lines

Step 4 — Can You See the Lines?

A circle full of fine diagonal lines will appear in the centre of the screen.

Your only job is to answer one simple question each trial:

● Yes, I can — you can see lines in the circle clearly
● No, I can't — lines look blurry or you can't see them

The lines will get finer or coarser based on your answers.
~10 trials total — trust your first impression.

Keyboard shortcuts: Y = Yes · N = No · Keep your covered eye covered

Step 5 — Far Point (Meridian 1)

After tapping Start, a circle of lines will appear on screen.

  1. Start close to the screen — lines should look clear
  2. Slowly lean back until the lines blur or disappear
  3. When they're just barely blurry — tap Lock in position
