Step 1 — Find Astigmatic Axis

You'll see two sets of lines appear one after the other.
Press 1 if the first looked sharper, or 2 if the second did.

There are no right or wrong answers — trust your instinct.

~6 comparisons · Keep your head still · Lines will appear in blue

Step 2 — Resolution Test

Fine lines will appear on the LEFT or RIGHT side.
Press ← Left or → Right (or tap the buttons) to say which side.
The test alternates between two orientations — just say where you see lines.

10 trials total · Both meridians interleaved · ~21 seconds

Step 3 — Far Point (Meridian 1)

Sit close to the screen (~30 cm) so the lines are clearly visible.
Then slowly lean back until the lines blur or disappear.
Hold still at that distance — it will auto-capture.
